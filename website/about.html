About VOIP@ Cloud

We are on a mission to make enterprise-grade cloud communications accessible to every business, everywhere. From startups to large enterprises, VOIP@ Cloud provides the tools you need to connect with your customers and teams.

Our Story

VOIP@ Cloud was founded with a simple idea: businesses should not need expensive hardware, complex installations, or long-term contracts to get a professional phone system. Traditional PBX systems were costly, rigid, and difficult to scale. We set out to change that.

Built on top of proven open-source telephony technologies, our platform delivers a fully-featured cloud PBX that provisions in minutes, not weeks. With your own domain, unlimited scalability, and a modern dashboard, VOIP@ Cloud puts you in control of your communications infrastructure.

Today, we serve hundreds of businesses across 50+ countries, handling thousands of calls every day. Whether you need 5 extensions or 5,000, our platform scales with you.

Our Mission

To democratize enterprise communications by providing affordable, reliable, and feature-rich cloud PBX solutions that any business can deploy in minutes.

Our Vision

A world where every business, regardless of size or location, has access to the same powerful communication tools as Fortune 500 companies.
